About us

Welcome to our inaugural group project, crafted with passion by the bright minds of Oulu University of Applied Sciences (OAMK) IT students Helmi Griffiths, Afsaneh Heidari, Natsuki Mine, Congying Zhao and Indunil Maheshika Liyanage. Here, we proudly present our endeavor: a heartwarming platform dedicated to pet adoption. Within these digital walls, we aim to connect loving homes with furry companions in need. Our mission is not just about facilitating adoptions; it's about fostering bonds that transcend species, enriching lives on both ends of the leash.

From playful pups to gentle felines, our site showcases a diverse array of animals eagerly awaiting their forever families. Each wagging tail and purring friend has a unique story and a hopeful gaze, ready to become an integral part of your life's journey. Join us in this noble pursuit of compassion and companionship. Together, let's make a difference, one paw print at a time.
